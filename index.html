About Me

Hi, Welcome to my Website! The above slide show has images of every country I have lived in. I was born in the Coventry, United Kingdom on March 19th 2000 after my parents moved there from Ferrol, Spain. We moved to London shortly after that and lived there until 2010. During the summer of 2010 we moved to Tashkent, Uzbekistan where we lived for over 2 years before moving to Rio de Janeiro, Brazil in January of 2013. My family lived in Rio for three years and in the summer of 2016 we moved back to the UK where I graduated from high school (Sixth Form) with my IB diploma at Dartford Grammar School. I then moved to Davenport, Iowa in the United States to pursue a BSc in Computer Science and a BA in International Business at St. Ambrose University where I currently attend (GO BEES!).
